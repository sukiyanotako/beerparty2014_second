BeerParty!

What's party?

This Years “Beer party 2014” of the Alumni association of the Osaka University of Foreign Studies is going to be held on Saturday, 26th of July. Let’s enjoy the now customary summer event “The OUFS Beer Party” together.
Please come and join the one and only hospitable and comfortable atmosphere of the Osaka University of Foreign Studies related Beer Party this year!
We are looking forward to see you on 26th of July.
